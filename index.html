About Share Autos in Mumbai

Share autos, also known as shared auto-rickshaws, are a popular and affordable mode of transport in Mumbai. Unlike regular auto-rickshaws that serve individual passengers, share autos follow fixed routes and pick up multiple passengers heading in the same direction. They operate from designated stands and follow specific routes connecting major neighborhoods, railway stations, and commercial areas.

Typical fares for share autos range from ‚Çπ10 to ‚Çπ30 per person, depending on the distance and route. The fare is significantly lower than hiring a private auto-rickshaw, making it an economical choice for daily commuters. Passengers pay a fixed rate based on the route, and the fare is collected either at the beginning or end of the journey. Most share auto stands display the route information and fare details on signboards.

To identify share auto stands, look for groups of auto-rickshaws parked at specific locations, often near railway stations, bus stops, or busy market areas. These stands typically have signboards indicating the destination and route number. Share autos usually wait until they have 4-6 passengers before departing, though this can vary by route.
